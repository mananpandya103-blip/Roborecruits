We are a Canadian automation operations partner that embeds directly inside your facility with trained technicians, engineers, and operational leads. We become part of your team — fluent in your systems, aligned with your KPIs, and accountable to your uptime.

What We Offer

Operational Services for
Automated Facilities

Every service we offer is designed around one goal: your automated warehouse running at full capacity, with minimal downtime, on every shift.

Go-Live Stabilization
30–90 Day Program

The first 30–90 days after go-live are the most critical and most vulnerable period for any automated warehouse. ROBO Recruits embeds directly with your team during commissioning and the post-launch window to prevent instability from becoming a pattern.

  • On-site presence during commissioning and cutover
  • System error response and escalation management
  • Operational documentation and runbook creation
  • Staff training and knowledge transfer
  • Daily reporting and performance tracking
Resident Automation Support
Ongoing Embedded

On-site automation technicians and engineers embedded within your facility for daily system health management. Your resident ROBO team owns first-response, preventive maintenance, and the documentation that reduces future downtime events.

  • Daily system health checks and preventive maintenance
  • First-response to errors, faults, and alerts
  • Ongoing documentation and SOP maintenance
  • Cross-shift knowledge continuity
  • Vendor liaison and escalation support
24/7 Operational Coverage
All-Shift Coverage

Automated warehouses don't stop at 5pm — and neither do we. ROBO Recruits provides shift-aligned supervision and technical responders across day, evening, and overnight operations.

  • Shift-aligned technical staffing
  • Formal handover protocols between shifts
  • Overnight and weekend coverage options
  • Priority response protocols for critical events
  • On-call escalation pathways
Performance Management
KPI & Optimization

Beyond keeping systems running, we actively drive performance improvement. Our team manages KPIs, analyzes throughput data, and implements changes that progressively increase your system's output and reliability.

  • KPI dashboard setup and daily tracking
  • Throughput and cycle time analysis
  • Root cause analysis for recurring failures
  • System configuration optimization
  • Monthly performance reporting
Peak & Expansion Support
Surge & Scale

Peak season, new facility expansions, and system upgrades all stress your automation in different ways. ROBO Recruits provides surge staffing and specialized operational support to ensure your system performs when stakes are highest.

  • Peak season staffing augmentation
  • Pre-peak system readiness assessments
  • Expansion commissioning support
  • System upgrade transition management
  • Rapid deployment for urgent coverage needs
